PELTI Flight Report for FF02, 23 July 2000

This was our ferry from Miami back to Jeffco. It was all flown at high altitude, in very clean air. We exposed two sets of Teflon filters on the flight. 


1305			Takeoff from MIA
1331			Level at 20,000'

~1335 - 1533		Teflon filter sample at 20,000', 87% of Isokinetic flow.
Extremely clean air!
The sample ended in cloud.

~1545 - ~1800	Teflon filter sample at 20,000'
			Brief cloud penetration early

1856			Landed at Jeffco
