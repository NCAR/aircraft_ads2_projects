    Sources of Uncertainty
    FSSP - No recording of housekeeping variables
    increased the uncertainties to > 20% in
    concentration and > 40% in LWC. Normally
    these uncertainties are ? 15% and 35%,
    respectively
    PVM - Underestimates water content in droplet
    sizes larger than 40 ?m. The PVM also
    appears to have zero drifts that are a result
    of aerosol sensitivity and possible
    alignment shifts. These have been taken
    out of the data in the processing.
    Hotwire - Under-estimates water content in droplet
    sizes larger than 40 ?m. Proximity to wing
    distorts airflow. Correction factors that
    have been applied are semi-empirical. Flow
    modeling studies are underway to compare
    with empirically derived flowrates.
